About
OpenMinTeD

OpenMinTeD is an EU-funded project that sets out to create an open, service-oriented ep-Infrastructure for Text and Data Mining (TDM) of scientific and scholarly content. This means that researchers can collaboratively create, discover, share and re-use Knowledge from a wide range of text-based scientific related sources in a seamless way. You are now at the beta-version of our platform. In the future it will be possible to run more services and tools and even to combine them to build your own workflow. For more information about the project visit www.openminted.eu.
