Want to register a new component?

We offer a number of ways for you to register your components. See them below and choose the one that suits you the most.

Do you want to provide us with the metadata of the component?

We have created a form for you so that you can easily provide us with the metadata that are needed and we can validate whether we have all the information that we need on the fly!

Do you have maven coordinates for your component?

Provide us with the artifactId and groupId and we will try to pre-fill as much information as we can for you!

Do you have an XML description of your component?

If you have an XML description of your component in the OMTD-schema, you can paste it below and we can register your component immediately!
